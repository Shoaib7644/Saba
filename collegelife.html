College Life

After completing my schooling in Oman, I returned to India to pursue higher education. It was a bittersweet moment—leaving behind a place I called home but stepping into a new world of opportunities. I enrolled in RIT for my engineering, where I spent four incredible years. Those college years were filled with endless assignments, sleepless nights before exams, and friendships that turned into family. Engineering life was a rollercoaster ride—full of challenges but also excitement as I explored my passion for technology. Eager to expand my knowledge, I then pursued my Master's from MIT Pune. This phase was even more transformative, shaping my career and giving me a deeper insight into my field. College life gave me some of the best memories, and it played a crucial role in making me who I am today.
